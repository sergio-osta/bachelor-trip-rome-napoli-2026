# Deep Research Itinerary — 11 Lads Bachelor Trip
## Rome (16–18) → Napoli (18–19) → Rome (19–20)

Prepared: 2026-03-03  
Group size: 11

---

## 1) Trip structure at a glance

- **Day 1 (16):** Arrival + Rome core + opening night
- **Day 2 (17):** Rome activity-heavy day (split options by vibe)
- **Day 3 (18):** Morning Rome + high-speed train to Napoli + Napoli night
- **Day 4 (19):** Napoli short block + return to Rome + split departures
- **Day 5 (20):** Chill Rome morning for remaining group + departures

Design goals:
- Keep each day with **standard plan + alternative cool plan**.
- Include options that are less overused tourist clichés.
- Avoid over-scheduling (11 people always moves slower).

---

## 2) Logistics and operating assumptions

## Group ops for 11

- Move in **2 subgroups** (5 + 6) for restaurants/bars.
- Appoint 3 rotating roles per day:
  - logistics lead (tickets/check-ins)
  - pace lead (timekeeping)
  - finance lead (shared spend tracking)
- Pre-book only high-risk items:
  - one premium dinner in Rome
  - one Napoli dinner
  - both intercity train legs

## Train reality (Rome ↔ Napoli)

High-speed routes (Italo/Frecciarossa) are usually around **1h10–1h20** between Termini and Napoli Centrale (plus boarding buffer).  
Practical planning buffer for 11:
- arrive station 30 min before
- same carriage if possible
- assume 2h15 door-to-door city center transfer window

(Use official Italo/Trenitalia for final schedule lock-in.)

## 3) Day-by-day full plan with alternatives

## DAY 1 — 16th (Rome) — "Land, lock in, launch"

### Standard plan (high success / low friction)

**Afternoon**
- Check-in + quick espresso orientation walk near base
- Group photo stop: Pincio terrace (sunset-friendly) or Gianicolo

**Evening**
- Dinner in Trastevere (book 19:30)
- Bar route: Trastevere → Campo de’ Fiori zone (2–3 bars max)

Why it works: minimal complexity on arrival day, immediate vibe.

### Alternative cool plan (less generic)

- **Speakeasy cocktail experience** (booked slots, dress tidy)
- Late-night Roman cellar jazz/bar pocket (Monti/Testaccio side)
- Optional urban game/scavenger format to break the ice (custom bachelor challenges)

### Night cap rules for Day 1
- End by 01:30 max to keep Day 2 strong.
- Everyone carries water and backup card/cash.

---

## DAY 2 — 17th (Rome) — activity-heavy bachelor core day

Run this day as **choose-your-track** depending on mood.

## Track A: Classic + quality

**Morning**
- Vatican area or Colosseum exterior + quick history-lite loop

**Lunch**
- Casual Roman classics (carbonara/amatriciana rotation)

**Afternoon activity options**
1. Vespa sidecar group tour (very bachelor-friendly for 11)
2. Pasta + spritz workshop in private room
3. Escape room / competitive game block

**Dinner**
- Steakhouse or shared tasting menu

**Night**
- Rooftop first drink + club option (pre-list if desired)

## Track B: Less-known / “cool local”

**Morning**
- EUR district architecture walk + photo content mission

**Lunch**
- Testaccio food market circuit

**Afternoon activity options**
1. Indoor skydiving (adrenaline option)
2. Natural wine crawl (small-group split then regroup)
3. Analog football/cinema nostalgia venue + betting game format

**Night**
- Speakeasy + late bites route, avoid over-committing to one club

## Track C: Sports + recovery hybrid

**Morning**
- 5-a-side football booking (or padel) for 90 minutes

**Afternoon**
- Thermal spa/recovery block (staggered slots)

**Night**
- Controlled dinner + one area crawl (Monti or Pigneto style)

## DAY 3 — 18th (Rome → Napoli) — transfer + Napoli ignition

### Standard plan

**Rome morning**
- Late breakfast
- Light activity only (no museum queues)

**Transfer**
- Train around midday/early afternoon to Napoli Centrale

**Napoli evening**
- Check-in
- Sunset walk: Lungomare / Castel dell’Ovo area
- Pizza benchmark dinner (book 2 nearby places as backup)
- Optional: Quartieri Spagnoli drinks route

### Alternative cool plan

- Before train: Rome aperitivo boat/river or bike loop
- Napoli: underground route focus (Napoli Sotterranea / catacombs style experience)
- Late: low-key local bars + pastry stop (sfogliatella/baba)

## DAY 4 — 19th (Napoli short block → Rome return + split departures)

Because some leave this day, keep it modular.

### Standard plan

**Morning Napoli (2–3h)**
- Street food sprint (cuoppo, fried pizza, espresso stops)
- One landmark loop near historic center

**Midday**
- Train back to Rome

**Afternoon Rome**
- Luggage strategy + airport transfer waves for same-day departures

**Evening for remaining subgroup**
- Better final dinner in Rome (non-tourist area)
- One celebratory bar, no overrun

### Alternative cool plan

- Replace city walk with **Napoli bay mini-boat** or quick coastal segment (weather dependent)
- Return Rome earlier for premium rooftop sunset farewell
