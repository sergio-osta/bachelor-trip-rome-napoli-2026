## 1) Critical decisions to lock now (in order)

1. **Intercity trains** (Rome→Napoli on 18th, Napoli→Rome on 19th)
2. **Accommodation location fit** (nightlife walkability + departure convenience)
3. **Two anchor dinners** (Rome + Napoli)
4. **One flagship activity per city** (bookable, weather-independent preferred)
5. **Departure split ops** (who leaves 19th vs 20th, luggage + airport waves)

If these 5 are locked, the trip is operationally safe.

---

## 2) Transport strategy (for 11 people)

## Intercity
- Use high-speed rail (Italo/Frecciarossa).
- For 11, treat each transfer as a 2h15 block door-to-door (buffer included).
- Buy seats together in one carriage if possible.

## In-city
- Move as 2 pods (5 + 6) for restaurants/bars.
- Rejoin at hard checkpoints every 90 min.
- Late-night returns: taxi/app only, never random street offers.

## Airport days (19/20 split)
- Pre-assign flights to Wave A / Wave B.
- One logistics lead per wave.
- Keep one backup transfer option if a taxi van no-shows.

- no regroup protocol at night
- late bookings for 11-person dinners
