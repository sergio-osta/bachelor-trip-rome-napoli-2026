### Pompeii practical notes
- Entry: ~€18 per person. Book skip-the-line for 12 — April Fridays can get busy.
- Opening hours (April): 09:00–19:00, last entry 17:30
- If you arrive ~15:00, you get ~3-4h which covers the key highlights (Forum, amphitheatre, House of the Faun, Via dell'Abbondanza, Garden of the Fugitives)
- The site is enormous (44 hectares) — don't try to see everything, pick a route
- Bring water. Wear comfortable shoes. Limited shade.
- Pompei Scavi station is right at the entrance (Porta Marina)

### Masterchef at the villa
- Need to buy ingredients — either in Naples before the train or at a local shop near Villa Cola
- Set up teams, competition format, judging panel
- Javi as head judge? Or Javi forced to cook the worst dish?

### Rule
Don't underestimate Pompeii fatigue — 3-4h of walking ruins in the sun. Masterchef at the house is the perfect low-energy evening.
