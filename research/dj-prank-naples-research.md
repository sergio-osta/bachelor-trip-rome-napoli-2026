# DJ Prank in Naples — Getting Javi Behind the Decks

**Goal:** Get Javi to DJ at a trashy/divey venue in Naples as a bachelor prank. Saturday April 18 evening.

---

## Is this realistic?

**Yes.** Naples nightlife is informal, cheap, and transactional. A group of 12 buying drinks all night is good business for a small bar. Cash in hand talks. The smaller and divier the bar, the more likely the owner IS the person behind the bar, making negotiation direct.

**The pitch (in Italian):** "È il suo addio al celibato! Possiamo farlo suonare per 15 minuti? Paghiamo noi!"
(It's his bachelor party! Can we let him play for 15 minutes? We'll pay!)

**Estimated bribe:** €50-100 cash + buying rounds for the bar. 60-70% success rate at dive bars.

---

## Top venue picks for the DJ stunt

### 1. Perditempo — Libri, vini e vinili (TOP PICK)
- **Address:** Vico San Pietro a Maiella 8, 80138 Napoli (off Via Tribunali, centro storico)
- **What it is:** Tiny bookshop/record store/bar that becomes a chaotic party zone on weekends. DJs play eclectic mixes on a minimal setup. Crowds spill into the street. Anarchic, hipster-dive energy.
- **Why it works:** Small, chaotic, informal. The DJ setup is a small corner, not a professional fortress. A group ordering drinks and offering €50-100 for a 15-min guest slot could absolutely work here.
- **Risk:** Could be too packed on Saturday. Staff/DJ may just say no.

### 2. Pepi Vintage Room (STRONG PICK)
- **Address:** Piazza San Domenico Maggiore, 80134 Napoli
- **Hours:** 6:00 PM - 2:00 AM daily
- **What it is:** "One part dive bar, one part speakeasy." Small, intimate, vintage sunglasses on the walls, no menu — bartender makes drinks by mood. Features DJ sets. Drinks ~€7.
- **Why it works:** Already has DJs. Small intimate space. Loose vibe. On a key nightlife square.

### 3. Velvet
- **Address:** Via Cisterna dell'Olio 11, Napoli (near Piazza Dante)
- **What it is:** Dark basement bar with live bands and DJ sets. Part of an emerging nightlife strip.
- **Why it works:** Basement dive + DJ format = loose enough for a stag do stunt.

### 4. Superfly Soulbar
- **Address:** Via Cisterna dell'Olio 12, Napoli
- **Hours:** 7 PM - 2:30 AM (until 4 AM weekends)
- **What it is:** Soul/funk bar. DJ plays 70s/80s/90s music. Vinyl/CD setup, not a professional booth. Positive vibes.
- **Why it works:** Retro DJ setup, small bar, the kind of place where a guest might spin a record.

### 5. Kinky Bar
- **Address:** Via Cisterna dell'Olio 16, Napoli
- **What it is:** Reggae bar on the same strip. Small, niche, laid-back.
- **Why it works:** Chill vibes = potentially open to letting someone have a go.

### 6. Colpa d'Alfredo — Pessime Abitudini
- **Address:** Centro storico area
- **What it is:** Tiny dive bar, cheap spritzes, cool cartoon-skull aesthetic. Google 4.7 rating.
- **Why it works:** Classic cheap dive. If there's any kind of speaker system, a cash offer could fly.

### 7. Alter Ego Lounge Bar
- **Address:** Via Santa Maria di Costantinopoli 105, Napoli (near Piazza Bellini)
- **Hours:** Sun-Wed 6:30 PM - 3:30 AM, Thu-Sat 6:30 PM - 4:00 AM
- **What it is:** Small lounge disco bar near Piazza Bellini. DJ sets, live music, art exhibitions.

## Karaoke backup (guaranteed humiliation, zero negotiation)

If no bar lets him DJ, pivot to karaoke.

### In central Naples

**21 Biergrube**
- **Address:** Via Piave 21, Vomero, Napoli
- **Hours:** 6 PM - 2 AM
- **What it is:** Pub + steakhouse with karaoke and live music on Fridays and Saturdays. Two levels: restaurant upstairs, stage with big screen downstairs.
- **Good for 12:** Yes, big enough. Saturday karaoke confirmed.

### Near Boscotrecase (if you don't want to go into central Naples)

**Sciuscia Club — Ercolano**
- **Address:** Via Viulio 2, Ercolano (~20 min Circumvesuviana from Boscotrecase)
- **What it is:** Discopub. Saturday format: dining (panino or pizza) + live music + karaoke show + animation + dance music from 23:30.
- **Why it works:** Close to base. Has karaoke AND a DJ after. Could negotiate Javi on the mic AND the decks.

**Club La Paranza — Ercolano**
- **What it is:** Neapolitan taverna with Saturday night show: karaoke, traditional music with tambourines, followed by disco with resident DJs.
- **Why it works:** Full entertainment package close to home. Traditional Neapolitan party vibe = hilarious for a bachelor group.

### Further out (only if desperate)

**Crazy Horse Pozzuoli**
- **Address:** Via Alfonso Artiaco 141, Pozzuoli
- **What it is:** All-you-can-eat and all-you-can-drink karaoke pub. From €25/person.
- **Downside:** Pozzuoli is 45-60 min from Boscotrecase. Not practical unless you're already in that direction.

**Taverna Senza Pensieri — Qualiano**
- **What it is:** Disco pub karaoke pizzeria. Explicitly markets for addio al celibato groups. Free karaoke + pizza.
- **Downside:** Qualiano is ~50 km from Boscotrecase. Too far.

- Street food in Quartieri Spagnoli / Spaccanapoli
- Cheap spritzes at Cammarota Spritz (€1 spritz) or Peppe Spritz at Piazza Bellini

- Head to Via Cisterna dell'Olio strip or Via Tribunali area

- Film everything
