# Private Pizza Chef at Villa Cola — Research

## Summary

A private chef comes to the villa in Boscotrecase and makes authentic Neapolitan pizza for the group of 11. This is a well-established service in the Naples/Campania region — multiple platforms and independent chefs cover the Vesuvius area.

**Why this works for us:**
- No transport logistics (everyone stays at the villa)
- No reservation headaches for 11 at a famous pizzeria
- Hands-on pizza-making option = bachelor activity + dinner in one
- Chef handles shopping, cooking, serving, cleanup
- Can be combined with a chill house evening

## What's included (standard)

**Always included:**
- Chef shops for fresh ingredients at local markets
- Mise en place and prep at the villa kitchen
- Cooking and serving
- Kitchen cleanup after the event
- Chef's time (typically 3–5 hours total)

**Usually included:**
- Multiple pizza varieties (margherita, marinara, diavola, etc.)
- Antipasti before the pizzas
- Dessert (sweet pizza or local dolce)

**Sometimes included (varies):**
- Wine or drinks (often an add-on)
- Portable pizza oven if the villa lacks one
- Hands-on pizza-making instruction
- Table setup

**Typically NOT included:**
- Tableware/dishes (villa expected to have these)
- Drinks/wine (unless specified)
- Gratuity
- Additional waitstaff for 10+ guests (some recommend this)

---

## Important: pizza oven logistics

Most private chefs assume a kitchen with standard oven. For proper Neapolitan pizza, you want high heat. Options:

1. **Chef brings a portable pizza oven** — some chefs have Ooni-style or electric pizza ovens. Ask when booking.
2. **Villa has a wood-fired oven** — check with Raffaello Saltapicchio whether Villa Cola has one.
3. **Chef uses the villa's regular oven** — works but won't be the full Neapolitan experience.

**Action item:** Contact villa host and ask if there's an outdoor oven or space to set up a portable one.
