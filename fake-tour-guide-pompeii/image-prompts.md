# Image Prompts for Fake Tour Guide Visual Aids

Print these at the LOWEST possible quality. Ideally on a home inkjet printer running
low on ink, then photograph the printout with a phone, then print THAT. The worse
they look, the funnier the bit. Fold them a few times and keep them in a manila folder
labeled "MATERIALE DIDATTICO — USO INTERNO".

---

## Instructions for maximum low quality

1. Generate the image with AI
2. Screenshot it (don't download — screenshot to lose quality)
3. Paste into a Word doc, stretch it to fill the page
4. Print in "draft" or "economy" mode
5. Photograph the printout at an angle, slightly blurry
6. Print THAT photo
7. Optional: photocopy it once for extra degradation

"A technical cross-section diagram of a Roman aqueduct but the water channel is colored

### 6. Pigeon Postal System Dovecote

scroll. Made in what looks like early 2000s PowerPoint with gradient backgrounds."

### 7. The Gladiator Gym with Mirrors
"Interior of a Roman amphitheatre with obvious Photoshop reflections added to the

### 11. The Prophetic Graffito

### 12. Coloured Stone Voting Urns
